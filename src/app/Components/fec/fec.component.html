Fresher Employment Centre

A CSR initiative by T&P Edutech to support freshers and recruiters alike.

As a part of the Corporate Social Responsibility, T&P has established Freshers' Employment Center (FEC), a platform that will ensure a win-win situation for freshers and recruiters alike. It is an organized effort for freshers seeking employment and companies seeking recruits. Freshers can enroll with FEC and will receive orientation and job opportunity updates at no cost. Companies can also register to hire trained fresh talent screened by T&P.

Desired Qualification:

BE / BTech / MCA / MCS / MSc / BCA / BSc / BCS / MBA etc.
